Honeypot under Honeyd

   

This is a low interaction honeypot, i.e. they don't provide real services, they just only to simulate them through scripts. This open source application gives the possibility to emulate a network entire computer, composed of different virtual operating systems that are capable of providing fictitious services. The principle of the Honeyd program is simple: when an intruder tries to connect to the IP address of the emulated system, Honeyd impersonates this system and begins communication with the intruder's computer.
